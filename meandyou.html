M E & Y O U

The silver arrow of love pierces my heart when we kiss.

The smoke of the red-hot blaze twirls through the trees.

We watch the night mist as it whispers through the air of foredoomed love.

The lake ripples in the gentle breeze that caresses it.

We go silent as a howl cuts through the quiet night air.

The sparks from the fire illuminate your eyes

Full of love,

Compassion,

And caring.

Full of devotion and hope.

We watch a fox slink past,

You are like a secret,

Something for me to cherish and keep to myself.
