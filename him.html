H I M

I love his passion for gaming,

His smile when he catches me staring,

His eyes when he watches me.

I love when he holds me,

When he tells me he loves me,

When he pulls me into his lap.

I love that he's organized,

That he's smart,

That he thinks of me.

I love his hands,

When I have to stand on my tiptoes to kiss him,

When we go for walks.

I love that he cares about me,

That he asks me about my day,

That he tells me how he feels.

I love that he respects me,

That he doesn't pressure me,

That he doesn't push me.

I love everything about him.

Though sometimes that scares me.

I don't want to be hurt again.

To be tossed aside again.

To be cheated on again.

To be forgotten again.

I don't want my love to go to waste,

Not again.

It's hard trusting him with my feelings,

Though I still do.

I just worry because

I love him.
